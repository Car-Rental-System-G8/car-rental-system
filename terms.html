Rental Terms & Conditions

Eligibility Requirements

To rent a vehicle from HV Car Rental, customers must:

  • Be at least 21 years of age (25 for premium vehicles)
  • Possess a valid driver's license held for at least 1 year
  • Present a major credit card in the renter's name
  • Provide proof of insurance or purchase our insurance coverage

Reservation & Payment

Reservations can be made online, by phone, or in person. A valid credit card is required to secure any reservation. The full rental amount will be charged at the time of pickup. We accept all major credit cards, but do not accept cash, debit cards, or prepaid cards for the security deposit.

Security Deposit: A security deposit of $200-$500 (depending on vehicle category) will be held on your credit card during the rental period. This amount will be refunded upon vehicle return, subject to the condition assessment.

Fuel Policy

All vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel and should be returned with a full tank. If the vehicle is returned with less fuel than at pickup, a refueling charge will apply at the current market rate plus a $25 service fee.

Mileage Policy

Standard rentals include 200 miles per day. Additional mileage will be charged at $0.35 per mile. Unlimited mileage packages are available at an additional cost.

Insurance Policy

Coverage Options

HV Car Rental offers several insurance coverage options to protect you during your rental period:

  • Basic Coverage (Included): Includes liability coverage up to state minimum requirements
  • Standard Protection ($15/day): Includes liability coverage up to $50,000 and collision damage waiver with $1,000 deductible
  • Premium Protection ($25/day): Includes liability coverage up to $100,000, collision damage waiver with $500 deductible, and personal effects coverage
  • Full Coverage ($35/day): Includes all of the above plus zero deductible and roadside assistance

Personal Insurance

If you have personal auto insurance, it may provide coverage for rental vehicles. We recommend checking with your insurance provider before declining our coverage options. You will be required to provide proof of insurance if declining our coverage.

Note: Regardless of the coverage option selected, damage resulting from prohibited uses (off-road driving, reckless operation, etc.) will not be covered and will be the full responsibility of the renter.

Credit Card Coverage

Some credit cards offer rental car insurance when the entire rental is charged to the card. This coverage is typically secondary to your personal insurance. Check with your credit card company for details.

Cancellation Policy

Standard Reservations

Our cancellation policy for standard reservations is as follows:

  • 48+ hours before pickup: Full refund
  • 24-48 hours before pickup: 80% refund
  • 12-24 hours before pickup: 50% refund
  • Less than 12 hours before pickup: No refund

Prepaid Reservations

For prepaid reservations (discounted rates):

  • 7+ days before pickup: 90% refund
  • 3-7 days before pickup: 70% refund
  • 1-3 days before pickup: 40% refund
  • Less than 24 hours before pickup: No refund

Modification Policy: Reservations can be modified free of charge if done at least 24 hours before pickup, subject to vehicle availability. Rate differences may apply.

No-Show Policy

If you fail to pick up your rental vehicle within 2 hours of the scheduled pickup time without notifying us, it will be considered a no-show, and the full rental amount will be charged with no refund.

Damages & Liability

Vehicle Inspection

All vehicles undergo a thorough inspection before each rental. We document the vehicle's condition, including any existing damage. We encourage customers to participate in this inspection at pickup and note any concerns before leaving the premises.

Customer Liability

The renter is responsible for:

  • All damage to the vehicle during the rental period not covered by the selected insurance option
  • Loss or damage to vehicle keys, documents, or accessories
  • Interior damage, including stains, burns, or tears
  • Damage resulting from prohibited uses or negligence
  • Towing or recovery costs if not covered by roadside assistance

Important: Using the vehicle for prohibited purposes (off-road driving, towing, racing, etc.) voids all insurance coverage and makes the renter fully liable for any resulting damage.

Reporting Accidents

In case of an accident, theft, or damage to the vehicle:

  • Contact local authorities immediately if required by law
  • Notify HV Car Rental within 24 hours
  • Complete an accident report form (available in the vehicle's glove compartment)
  • Do not admit fault or liability at the scene
  • Collect information from other parties involved, including insurance details
